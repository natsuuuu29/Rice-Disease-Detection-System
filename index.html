Disease Information

Rice Blast Overview

  • Known to affect rice yield significantly
  • Typically appears as lesions on leaves
  • Highly contagious among plants
  • Can lead to complete crop loss in severe cases

Favorable Conditions

  • High humidity (above 90%)
  • Moderate temperatures (20-30°C)
  • Prolonged leaf wetness
  • Dense planting with poor air circulation

Prevention Methods

  • Use resistant rice varieties
  • Avoid excessive nitrogen fertilizer
  • Ensure proper field drainage
  • Apply fungicides preventatively
  • Practice crop rotation
